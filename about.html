About Us

Learn more about the Skill Swap Hub project

The Community Skill Swap Hub is a platform designed to help people share knowledge, resources, and hobbies. Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where everyone can learn something new and contribute their expertise.

Whether you’re into coding, baking, art, or exploring AI tools, this hub provides a space to connect with others who share your interests. You can post resources, comment on others’ contributions, and engage in meaningful discussions.

In future iterations, we plan to integrate secure authentication, user profiles, and a database to manage resources more effectively. For now, the focus is on building a clean, user-friendly front-end experience that demonstrates the concept.
